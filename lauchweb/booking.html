The Velvet Claw Ethos

Founded in 2018, The Velvet Claw emerged from a vision to redefine nail artistry in London. We combine technical excellence with creative innovation.

Our Mayfair atelier is designed as a sanctuary where clients experience meticulous craftsmanship in an environment of understated luxury.

Every detail - from our curated product selection to the ergonomic design of our stations - is considered to elevate your experience.
